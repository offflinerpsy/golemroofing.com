Redondo Beach Roofing FAQ

How much does a roof replacement cost in Redondo Beach?

A residential roof replacement in Redondo Beach typically ranges from $9,000 to $30,000+, depending on home size, material, and roof complexity. North Redondo tract homes tend to be on the lower end ($9,000–$15,000 for shingles), while larger South Redondo custom homes cost more. We provide free, detailed estimates.

Is my roof damaged from salt air?

Very likely if you're in South Redondo or near King Harbor. Salt air corrodes metal flashings, fasteners, and vents over time. During our free inspection, we check for signs of salt corrosion and recommend preventive measures — including marine-grade fasteners and corrosion-resistant flashings.

Do you service both North and South Redondo?

Yes. We serve all of Redondo Beach — both ZIP codes (90277 and 90278), from Aviation Boulevard to the beachfront. Same quality, same warranties, same crew.

How long does a roof replacement take in Redondo Beach?

Most residential replacements take 1–3 days. Larger homes or tile-to-tile replacements may take 3–5 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption and always leave a watertight roof at the end of each work day.
